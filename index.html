Short Bio

His has two ultimate goals in this life. One is to build a plausible 3D virtual world (Open 3D Virtual Reality, Open3DVR). The other is to use machine learning methods to solve biotechnologial problems in the future (Open Biotechnology, OpenBiotech).

Open3DVR and OpenBiotech are non-profit open research platforms, which focus on advancing technologies and benefit humanity.

Life is short. He will spend 30 years to develop related technologies to achieve these two goals. He will spend the first 10 years to develop Open3DVR (2023-2033) and spend the next 20 years (2033-2053) to develop OpenBiotech. All relevant researchers are welcome.
